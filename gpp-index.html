Government Publications Portal

The Government Publications Portal holds New York City’s recent agency publications. The portal is maintained by the Municipal Library at the New York City Department of Records and Information Services (DORIS) as part of the government’s ongoing mission to make government information accessible.

The New York City Charter, Section 1133, requires agencies to submit digital copies of all publications to the Library. A list of all required reports is published on this website. Should the agencies concerned not submit the report within the required time limit, DORIS will issue a request for the report to the agency. Such requests will be published on this website in place of the report until the report is published.

To find publications, search by keywords such as agency name, subject, title, type, or date.
