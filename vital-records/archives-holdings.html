Archives Holdings

The New York City Municipal Archives holds historical vital records for all 5 boroughs. The Historical Vital Records of NYC website publishes digitized versions of vital records from all 5 boroughs as of the 1898 consolidation that formed New York City. Pre-1898 records from Manhattan and Brooklyn (Kings) counties are also available on this website.

Certified legal copies of vitals and copies of vitals not yet mass-digitized (including many pre-1898 consolidation records) can be ordered online.

For active, non-historical records, please reach out to the City Clerk's Office.
