Archives Holdings

The New York City Municipal Archives holds historical vital records for all 5 boroughs. The Historical Vital Records of NYC website publishes digitized versions of vital records starting in 1898, when the boroughs consolidated to form New York City. Certified copies of vitals, copies of vitals not yet available on this website, and copies of pre-consolidation records in our holdings can be ordered online.
For active, non-historical records, please reach out to the City Clerk's Office.
